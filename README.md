In Tasmania, because the afternoon sun is hotter than the morning sun, the driest slope of a mountain is not directly north, but rather 20° offset anti-clockwise

When you look at hills and mountains you find that the vegetation on steep NNW slopes is significantly different to steep SSE slopes

This script takes a DEM and uses its slope and aspect to determine 'dryness' of Tasmanian slopes, for ecological analysis and other purposes
